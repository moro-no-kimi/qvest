## Fulton County School District
Fulton County Schools (FCS) is the fourth largest school system in Georgia with more than 10,900 full- and part-time employees, and more than 6,200 teachers and other certified personnel, who work in 102 schools and administrative support buildings.

During the 2025-26 school year, a projected enrollment of 86,031 students will attend classes in 56 elementary schools, 19 middle schools, 19 high schools, 7 start-up charter organizations (some charters have multiple campuses but are considered one school), and 1 full-time virtual school.

## Destiny Library Manager
Fulton County Schools uses Destiny Library Manager to streamline our library operations and provide numerous advantages to our students, staff, and community. We believe this technology will greatly enrich your child's educational experience.
 

Here are some of the key features and benefits of Destiny Library Manager:
 

Easy Access to Resources: Destiny Library Manager allows students to search for books, eBooks, and other educational materials quickly and efficiently. Parents can also access the system to check the availability of resources and assist their children in finding relevant materials for their studies.
Online Catalog: Our entire library catalog will be accessible online, making it convenient for students and parents to browse, reserve, and request materials from home or any internet-enabled device.
Enhanced Communication: The system enables us to send notifications and updates about library events, due dates, and other important information directly to your email address, ensuring you stay informed and engaged with your child's library activities.
Remote Learning Support: During times of remote or hybrid learning, Destiny Library Manager will provide remote access to our library resources, ensuring that your child has access to the materials they need for their studies.

## Identity and Productivity Platform

Fulton County Schools operates a district-wide Microsoft 365 enterprise agreement. All students K-12 have access to Microsoft 365 applications including Outlook, Teams, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and OneDrive.

- Student email addresses follow the format `ID#@fcstu.org` where the student ID is the Fulton lunch number
- Students access district applications via ClassLink SSO at `launchpad.classlink.com/fcs`
- Microsoft SharePoint is used for internal employee resources (Employee Hub at `fultonk12.sharepoint.com/sites/employeehub`)
- The district's agreement with Microsoft for Microsoft 365 is FERPA compliant
- Source: https://www.fultonschools.org/all-departments/academics/learning-teaching/instructional-technology/microsoft-365

Any new application built for the district must integrate with Microsoft Entra ID (formerly Azure AD) as the identity provider and surface via ClassLink SSO where feasible. Introducing a separate identity silo is not acceptable given Dr. Phillips' documented focus on reducing application sprawl.
